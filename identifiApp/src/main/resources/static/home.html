Welcome to IdentiFi

The diagnosis process resembles that of solving a rubrik's cube: a constellation of variables must align just the right way. IdentiFi helps you and your healthcare provider achieve accurate diagnosis by helping you keep a documentation of your symptoms and activities. The goal to help you see any pattern that indicates a correlation between activities and symptoms, so that you can identify any triggers.
